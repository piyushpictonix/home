Our Heritage

400 Years of
Nawabi Craft

Chikankari is not just embroidery — it is the soul of Lucknow. Introduced by Nur Jahan in the Mughal era, each stitch is a testament to patience, artistry, and the timeless elegance of the City of Nawabs. Our karigars have inherited this art through generations, and we bring it to you, uncompromised.
